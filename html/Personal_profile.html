WESTERN FOODS


Western food, which is a general term for Western-style meals, mainly refers to foods and drinks that are sourced from Western countries and eaten with cutlery. Today, with the increasingly close contact between China and foreign countries, receptionists often face the problem of participating in western food banquets.
